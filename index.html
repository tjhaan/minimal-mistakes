About Me:

Hello and welcome to my academic website! I am currently a graduate student at the University of Alaska Fairbanks pursuing my M.S. in Biological Sciences. My thesis work delves into how disturbance to permafrost soils affects the types, abundance, and mobility of antibiotic resistance genes in soil microbial communities of the active layer.

More broadly I am interested in one health, which is the intersection between animal, human, plant, and environmental wellbeing, and how we can use data science like genomics to address health challenges.
